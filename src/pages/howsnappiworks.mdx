# How snappi works?


Snappi offers a range of APIs that enable seamless financial transactions and services for various use cases. Whether you’re facilitating payments, checking account information, or integrating with other financial platforms, Snappi’s APIs ensure smooth and secure operations across different environments.

You can leverage Snappi for:

	•	Real-time payments and settlements
	•	Secure authentication and authorization through PSD2 compliance
	•	Custom integrations to enhance your financial services

Snappi provides an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for developers and businesses of all sizes to integrate the API into their platforms. The layout is designed for easy navigation, whether you’re handling single transactions or building complex financial workflows.
